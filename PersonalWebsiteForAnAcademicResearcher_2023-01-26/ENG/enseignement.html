TEACHING EVALUATION

To expand my teaching skills, in recent years, I have observed experienced professors in action and followed numerous training sessions in applied pedagogy.

These efforts have borne fruit as in the four most recent evaluations of my teaching, in response to the statement “Generally speaking, I would recommend this instructor to other students”, my students have given me an average grade of 3.87/4.00, noting the passion that animates my teaching as well as my ability to explain course material in a simple, practical, and integrated manner.
