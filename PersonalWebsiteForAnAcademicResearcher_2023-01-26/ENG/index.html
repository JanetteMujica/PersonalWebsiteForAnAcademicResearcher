biography

After completing a bachelor’s degree in business administration specialized in applied economics (BBA, 2005), I worked for 8 years as in analyst and strategic advisor in institutional investments for large pension plans. In parallel, I completed the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA 2009) and a master’s degree in business administration (MBA 2014). Then, I moved on from institutional investment to complete a doctoral degree in management at HEC Montreal (PhD 2019) under the supervision of Prof. Luciano Barin-Cruz.

I am also cofounder of Elsewhere Consulting, where we facilitate the implementation of collective learning processes inspired by participatory action-research to unlock the agency and systemic influence of supported communities.

I am now a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Ottawa under the supervision of Prof. Samia Chreim, holding a 2-year SSHRC funding.
